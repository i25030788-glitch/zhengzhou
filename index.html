The Cradle of
Civilization

Situated on the southern bank of the Yellow River, Zhengzhou is one of the Eight Great Ancient Capitals of China.

It served as the capital of the Shang dynasty over 3,600 years ago. Today, it stands as a bustling national transportation hub, seamlessly weaving historical wonders with modern urban expansion.

Iconic Landmarks

Journey through time with these extraordinary sites.

Shaolin Temple

The legendary birthplace of Chinese Zen Buddhism and globally renowned Shaolin Kung Fu.

Yellow River Area

Majestic views of the mother river of China, featuring the colossal statues of Emperors Yan and Huang.

Henan Museum

A treasure trove of antiquities housing over 130,000 cultural relics shaped like an ancient observatory.

Taste of Zhongyuan

Hui Mian (Braised Noodles)

You cannot visit Zhengzhou without tasting its signature dish. A rich, nourishing bowl of mutton soup with hearty, hand-pulled flat noodles, kelp, tofu threads, and fresh coriander.

Yu Opera

Experience the passion of Henan through Yu Opera, one of China's most famous regional opera forms known for its loud, sonorous singing and rhythmic energy.
